SHADE NET

Shade net is a knitted HDPE material used in different Applications but it is main application is in agriculture. It is used to Safeguard agricultural produce from Environmental adverse effects like intense sunlight, strong winds, heavy rain etc. It Helps to reduce wastage of crops and thus increase its productivity. It is commonly used to cover the agricultural fields and also Helpful for Washing and Boiling Process of Turmeric (Haldi).

It is also used in nurseries to make sure the plant growth is steady and uninterrupted. It also be used to ensure safety at work in process and to restrict wastes from spilling at the construction site. Commonly, people use Shade Net to cover vehicles to protect them heating in hot summer. It also used in Parking Lot to provide shade. It is UV stabilized which prevents UV rays .

Available in 1m, 2m ,3m ,4m ,5m & 6 meters width with 50%, 75%, & 90% Shading Percentages and Packed in form of Bales as well in form of Rolls.
