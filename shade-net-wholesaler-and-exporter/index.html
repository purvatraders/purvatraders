SHADE NET

Shade net is a knitted HDPE material used in different Applications but it is main application is in agriculture. It is used to Safeguard agricultural produce from Environmental adverse effects like intense sunlight, strong winds, heavy rain, etc. It helps to reduce wastage of crops and thus increase their productivity. It is commonly used to cover the agricultural fields 

It is also used in nurseries to make sure the plant growth is steady. 

It also is used to ensure safety at work in process and to restrict wastes from spilling at the construction site. Commonly, people use Shade Net to cover vehicles to protect them heating in hot summer. It is also used in the Parking Lot to provide shade. It is UV stabilized which prevents UV rays.

Purva Traders is the best Quality Shade Net Wholesaler From Maharashtra. We Supply Shade net of Various varieties, sizes, and colors according to buyers’ requirements. It is made with 100% pure virgin HDPE Granules which make it strong as well as Lightweight. 

Available in 1m, 2m,3m,4m,5m & 6 meters width with 50%, 75%, & 90% Shading Percentages and Packed in form of Bales as well in form of Rolls.

Applications of Shade Net

In Making of Agro Green House

Shade Net is popularly useful for making Green House for agricultural purposes. Green House provides the ideal conditions required for the growth of plants.

For Parking

Shade net used in parking lots to provide shade to vehicles prevent them from heating and also protect them from UV rays which can fade their colors.

For Boundary Fencing

It can be a cheap solution that can provide boundary protection.

At Construction Sites

Shade Nets are used to provide extra safety to the workers as well as the passers-by. Also helps to reduce wastage caused due to spilling.

For Terrace Garden

It Is useful for making terrace gardens which helps in providing Ideal conditions That are required for plant growth.
