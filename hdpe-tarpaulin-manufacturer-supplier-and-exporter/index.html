HDPE TARPAULINS

HDPE tarpaulins are made up of Premium Non-woven HDPE fabric which is then laminated with LDPE coat from both top and bottom sides. Tarpaulins are available in various Thicknesses and Qualities. Lamination of Polymer Coat on it Make it Waterproof, Wear and tear proof, Strong , Durable and Easy to use for rough and Tuff Purposes. 

As Polymer is Used for lamination as well as For Making its Fabric It becomes Lightweight and 100% Waterproof as well as Moisture and Fungi Resistant. Hence, Making HDPE tarpaulins Very Useful To Protect Agricultural Produce From getting Wet By Rain as well as from other harsh Conditions Hence Reducing spoilage of Agricultural Produce .
