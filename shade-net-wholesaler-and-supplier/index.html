Shade net is a knitted HDPE material used in different Applications but it is main application is in agriculture. It is used to Safeguard agricultural produce from Environmental adverse effects like intense sunlight, strong winds, heavy rain etc. It Helps to reduce wastage of crops and thus increase its productivity. It is commonly used to cover the agricultural fields 

It is also used in nurseries to make sure the plant growth is steady. 

It also be used to ensure safety at work in process and to restrict wastes from spilling at the construction site. Commonly, people use Shade Net to cover vehicles to protect them heating in hot summer. It also used in Parking Lot to provide shade. It is UV stabilized which prevents UV rays .

Applications of Shade Net

In Making of Agro Green House

Shade Net is popularly  useful for making Green House for agricultural purposes. Green House provide the ideal conditions required for growth of plants.

For Parking

Shade net used in parking lots to provide shade to vehicles and prevent them from heating and also protect them from UV rays which can fade their colors.

For Boundary Fencing

It can be a cheap solution which can provide boundary protection.

At Construction Sites

Shade Nets are used to provide extra safety to the workers as well as the passers-by. Also helpful to reduce wastage caused due to spilling.

For Terrace Garden

It Is useful for making terrace garden which helpful in providing Ideal conditions That are required for plant growth.
